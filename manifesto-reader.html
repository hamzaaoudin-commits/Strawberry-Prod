Scene 04 · Resistance
Anti-AI
005
Strawberry Prod.
Scene 04 — Narrative Immunity
The four layers.
Why the machine can imitate everything — except this.
L · 01
Taste / goût éditorial
AI optimizes. It doesn't choose. Strawberry knows what to remove — not just what to add. Perfection is the subtraction of the obvious.
L · 02
Tension / architecture émotionnelle
AI generates smooth content. Comfortable. Dead. We engineer micro-discomforts that force attention. Example — "You're not ready for this." A hook that repels and attracts.
L · 03
Subtext / ce qui n'est pas dit
AI writes literally. We write in subtext. A Strawberry line operates on three levels: the explicit message, the implicit positioning, the cultural identity.
L · 04
Aura / présence intangible
AI can copy a style. It cannot create a presence. Each project carries a unique vibrational signature. Main Character Energy — impossible to standardize.
